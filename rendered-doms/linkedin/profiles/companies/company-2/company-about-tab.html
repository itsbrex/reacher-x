Overview

Mux is video infrastructure that makes it easy for developers to build video into their products, and do so quickly, reliably, and at global scale. Thousands of companies like Substack, HubSpot, Vimeo, Paramount, and PBS rely on Mux’s APIs to deliver the highest quality video experiences, and monitor what’s happening across every frame along the way.
