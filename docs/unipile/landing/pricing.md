## FAQs

#### What is the cost to start using Unipile's API ?

Unipile offers a tiered pricing model, where the cost per account decreases as the number of linked accounts increases. It’s important to note that an “account” refers to **1 single LinkedIn**, **1 single WhatsApp**, or **1 single email address**. For example, if you link a LinkedIn account and a WhatsApp account, this will count as **2 separate accounts**.

- For **up to 10 linked accounts**, the base price is **49€/$55 per month**.
- For **11 to 50 linked accounts**, the cost is **5.00€/$5.50 per account per month**.
- For **51 to 200 linked accounts**, the price decreases to **4.50€/$5.00 per account per month**.
- For **201 to 1000 linked accounts**, the rate is **4.00€/$4.50 per account per month**.
- For **1001 to 5000 linked accounts**, the cost is further reduced to **3.50€/$4.00 per account per month**.
- For **5001+ linked accounts**, the lowest rate applies at **3.00€/$3.50 per account per month**.

For example, if you have **15 linked accounts**, your total cost will be **75€/$82.50** (15 accounts × 5.00€/5.50$ each).

We operate on a post-paid model: at the end of each monthly consumption period, we’ll generate an invoice based on the total number of linked accounts you had during that month. Your first invoice will be issued at the end of your first 30-day period.

You can [start](https://dashboard.unipile.com/signup) using Unipile’s API services **for free** with a **7-day trial**. No credit card is required, and during this period, you’ll have full access to all features and functionalities, giving you the flexibility to explore our services without any commitment.

#### Is Unipile’s API DMA compliant?

Yes. The Digital Markets Act (DMA) is a European law, and Unipile ensures its API design aligns with its interoperability and data access principles. Our integrations are built to support compliant connections with platforms under the DMA framework.

#### Is Unipile SOC 2 certified?

Unipile’s infrastructure and internal processes are aligned with SOC 2 requirements, ensuring enterprise-grade data protection and operational reliability.  
The SOC 2 certification audit is completed, with official compliance confirmation effective **October 19, 2025**.

#### Do I need to follow Meta’s Terms of Service when using Unipile?

Absolutely. You must always respect Meta’s TOS, using the API responsibly, avoiding spam or unauthorized automation, and focusing only on legitimate messaging use cases.

#### Are the prices degressive based on volume?

Yes, prices are degressive based on the volume of accounts linked. For more detailed pricing information, please [contact us](/contact-us/) directly.

#### Can I integrate unlimited accounts with instant messaging providers ?

Yes, you can integrate accounts with various instant messaging providers. However, pricing is based on the number of accounts linked.

#### What platforms does Unipile support?

Unipile supports integration with various platforms for messaging, including WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Instagram, Messenger, Telegram, and X (Twitter). Additionally, Unipile supports email providers such as Gmail, Outlook, and IMAP.

#### Are there any usage limits with Unipile’s API?

Unipile does not enforce any limits on your usage, you can send and receive as many messages or events as you need.  
However, each provider (like LinkedIn, Gmail, WhatsApp, etc.) may apply its own restrictions (rate limits, quotas, usage policies).  
We fully respect these limits and recommend reviewing them here:  
👉 [Provider Limits and Restrictions](https://developer.unipile.com/docs/provider-limits-and-restrictions)

#### What functionalities are provided by the Messaging API Integration?

The Messaging API Integration provides targeted outreach, data enrichment, and inbox synchronization features to enhance your communication capabilities.

#### Are the prices mentioned inclusive of VAT?

No, the prices mentioned are exclusive of VAT. VAT will be added separately to the total cost as applicable.
