# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

ReacherX is meant to be a respectful, constructive, and welcoming project.

By participating, you agree to help make the community safe for people with different backgrounds, identities, skill levels, and perspectives.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- being respectful in disagreement
- assuming good intent while still being honest and direct
- giving actionable feedback instead of hostile criticism
- helping new contributors find context
- respecting boundaries, time, and privacy

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or conduct
- intimidation, dogpiling, or bad-faith behavior
- publishing someone else's private information without permission
- sexualized language or unwanted attention
- repeated disruption of discussion or review processes

## Maintainer Expectations

ReacherX is a collaborative project, but product direction still matters.

Salman may redirect discussions, close proposals, or ask for feature discussions to move to direct maintainer contact when a change affects roadmap, product strategy, agent behavior, or architecture.

That is not meant to shut people down. It is part of keeping the project coherent.

Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior, including warnings, content removal, blocking, or banning from project spaces.
